Hexaterrestrials

Turn-based action builder where character's attributes are based on which side he lands on three sided arenas

Portfolio | About Me

Features

  • 2-4 players
  • Map editor
  • Controller support

Gameplay

  • Play as a cube
  • Throw yourself around the map
  • Shoot enemies or use tools depending on which side you land
  • Gatling guns, missiles, wall builders
  • Physics based, planet has a gravitation and takes you around the back sides if you jump or get tossed there
